Billing department policies.

Billing Requests

  • To require an invoice, you must fill out the computer billing format with all the required data correctly.
  • The invoice request format must be submitted by appending student CURP and taxpayer RFC.
  • Append student INE
  • The format must be sent before making your payment to the billing factura@universidadazteca.edu.mx
  • No invoices are made for payments prior to the billing request date. The amount that will come on the invoice is for which it was made (in a single exhibition)
  • Only invoices are made for payments made.
  • When the payment is made, it is charged to the system, issuing a provisional receipt (no tax value) as proof of payment. Which must be signed and sealed by the box department, the seal must be in blue. Otherwise, it will have no value.
  • When a student does not require an invoice, a global invoice is issued.

Cancellations

  • It is important to clarify that the invoice will be issued with the new version 3.3 where invoices can no longer be canceled.
  • To cancel a receipt, the customer must give direct authorization on the SAT portal.

SUCA BILLING (SUSPENSION, CANCELLATION, CHANGE) FF2

  • In case the student wishes to suspend, cancel or modify any data the format SUCA invoice will be sent, (the link you will find at the bottom of this page), to the following factura@universidadazteca.edu.mx it is important to carry out the procedure immediately.
